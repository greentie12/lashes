Policies

What you should know

Payment & Deposit

Payment for services rendered are to be paid in CASH only on the day of the appointment.

A $20 deposit will be required before booking your appointment via Cash App ($lashessbysteph). This amount does go towards your remaining balance. Deposit is non-refundable and you are not able to use this towards another appointment if you cancel.

Refunds

We do not refund services. If you have any concerns with the services provided please notify us immediately. We offer a one-time complimentary follow-up appointment that is limited to 60 minutes. A one-time complimentary follow-up appointment must be made within 72 hours of the initial appointment.

No Shows

If you no call/no show you will be removed from booking any future services with us. You will also be charged 50% of the service scheduled.

Cancellations

We require a 24-hour notice for any cancellation / rescheduling. If you do cancel or would like to reschedule your appointment under the 24-hour notice you will be charged a $25 fee. To avoid this from happening please cancel / reschedule your appointment when you receive the reminder text / email within 24 hours prior to your appointment.

Late / Early Arrivals

Please notify us if you are running late. If you are 15 minutes late a $10 fee will be added to your appointment. However, complete coverage is not guaranteed. If you are 25 minutes late your appoinment will be cancelled and a $25 fee will be required before you can book any further appoinments with us.

Please notify us if you are arriving before your scheduled appointment as you may have to wait in your vehicle. If we can begin services pior to your appointment time you will be notified.

Extra Guest / Children

Please refrain from bringing any extra guests to your appoinment if they're not being serviced. Due to minimal space, our waiting area seating is limited.

Due to liability concerns, we do noy allow children into the studio during your service. No Exceptions.
